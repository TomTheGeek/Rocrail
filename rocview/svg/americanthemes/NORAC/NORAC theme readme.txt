Generic NORAC theme not applicable to any particular railroad but suitable where nothing else exists. Based on SpDrS60 theme. The following signal masts are supported:-

Prefix	Mast type
	Single head searchlight mast automatic
	twin head searchlight mast automatic
1a_	Single head searchlight mast standard head aspect numbers
1_	Single head searchlight mast unified NORAC aspect numbers
2_	Twin head searchlight mast unified NORAC aspect numbers
3_	Triple head searchlight mast unified NORAC aspect numbers

For position light masts look at the member railroads that use them - Amtrak, B&O and PRR.

Semaphore has been used to give a twin head automatic alternative to the single head automatic.
